About

Wisterlitz is a collaboration between artists Liz Lister and Rebecca Hurwitz. Since 2015, we have explored the connections between science, technology, and society through a feminist perspective. Our projects create spaces where people can encounter new ideas, consider different viewpoints, and engage with curiosity, disagreement, play, and reflection. We’re developing an approach rooted in dialogic space—places where people, objects, and ideas come together for genuine exchange. These spaces encourage audiences to slow down, question assumptions, and look at topics from angles they might not usually explore.
